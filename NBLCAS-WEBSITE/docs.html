About

NBLCAS (NBL Contract and Statistics) is an independent fan-built resource for tracking Australian NBL player contracts and statistics. Data is sourced from publicly available information and is updated on a best-effort basis.

Contracts data is sourced from SpatialJam. Player statistics are sourced from the Historical Stats Database on Tableau.

NBLCAS is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with the National Basketball League of Australia.
