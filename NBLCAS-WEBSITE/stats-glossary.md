# Stats Glossary

Definitions for every advanced statistic used in the NBL historical stats database.

---

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **3PR** | Three-Point Rate | The percentage of a player's field goal attempts that come from three-point range. |
| **AST%** | Assist Percent | An estimate of the percentage of teammate field goals a player assisted while on the floor. |
| **BLK%** | Block Percent | An estimate of the percentage of opponent two-point field goal attempts blocked by a player per 100 possessions. |
| **BPM** | Box Plus Minus | A box score-based metric that estimates a player's contribution to the team per 100 possessions, relative to a league-average player. |
| **DRE** | Daily RAPM Estimate | An estimate of Regularised Adjusted Plus Minus calculated from box score data, measuring a player's impact on both ends of the floor. |
| **dRAT** | Defensive Rating | An estimate of the number of points a team allows per 100 possessions while a player is on the floor. |
| **dREB%** | Defensive Rebound Percent | An estimate of the percentage of available defensive rebounds a player grabbed while on the floor. |
| **eFG%** | Effective Field Goal Percent | A shooting efficiency metric that adjusts field goal percentage to account for the fact that three-point field goals are worth more than two-point field goals. |
| **FTR** | Free Throw Rate | The ratio of free throw attempts to field goal attempts, indicating how often a player gets to the free-throw line. |
| **NetRAT** | Net Rating | The difference between a team's Offensive Rating and Defensive Rating while a player is on the floor, reflecting overall impact. |
| **oRAT** | Offensive Rating | An estimate of the number of points a team scores per 100 possessions while a player is on the floor. |
| **oREB%** | Offensive Rebound Percent | An estimate of the percentage of available offensive rebounds a player grabbed while on the floor. |
| **PACE** | Pace (Possessions) | The estimated number of possessions per 48 minutes while a player is on the floor, indicating the tempo of play. |
| **PER** | Player Efficiency Rating | A single-number summary of a player's per-minute productivity, standardised so that the league average is 15. |
| **PPS** | Points Per Shot | The average number of points scored per field goal attempt, factoring in two-pointers, three-pointers, and free throws. |
| **STL%** | Steal Percent | An estimate of the percentage of opponent possessions that end with a steal by this player while on the floor. |
| **TOV%** | Turnover Percent | An estimate of turnovers committed per 100 plays — lower is better for the player. |
| **tREB%** | Total Rebound Percent | An estimate of the percentage of all available rebounds (offensive and defensive) a player grabbed while on the floor. |
| **TS%** | True Shooting Percent | A measure of shooting efficiency that accounts for field goals, three-point field goals, and free throws. |
| **USG%** | Usage Rate | An estimate of the percentage of team plays used by a player while on the floor, via field goal attempts, free throws, and turnovers. |
| **VORP** | Value Over Replacement Player | Measures a player's total contribution relative to a replacement-level player, expressed as points per 100 team possessions. |
